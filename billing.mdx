FlexInference is **free to route**. The only thing you ever pay us is a commission on the flex requests that actually save you money - and even then, you keep the larger share.

## What you pay

FlexInference charges **20% of the money saved** on a flex request. A flex request is a duration [`start_within`](/deadline-routing) (the flex race) on a **priced, non-Anthropic model** - the flex-capable OpenAI and Gemini [models](/models).

The saving is the difference between the provider's standard price for that call and the flex price the race actually won. We take 20% of that difference as commission; you keep the other 80%. If a flex request saves nothing, there is nothing to commission.

<Note>
  Commission is charged on the **saving**, never on the tokens. You are always better off than paying the standard price: worst case the saving is zero and so is the commission.
</Note>

## What is free

- **All standard, `priority`, and `auto` routing**, on any model. These proxy straight to the provider with no flex race, so there is no saving to share and no commission.
- **All Anthropic (Claude) models.** Claude is proxy-only - it has no flex tier, so a `claude-*` request is never a billable flex request. See [models](/models).

Only a **priced flex request on an OpenAI or Gemini model** ever accrues commission.

## Bring your own key (BYOK)

FlexInference routes with **your** provider keys. Your provider (OpenAI, Gemini, or Anthropic) bills you **directly** for the tokens, at their rates. FlexInference adds **no markup** on tokens - we never touch your token spend. The only charge from FlexInference is the 20% flex commission described above.

## How your card is charged

Add or update a card on the dashboard **Billing** page. Commission accrues per flex request as you use the API, and:

- Your card is charged **automatically, monthly**, once accrued commission reaches **$20**.
- A balance **under $20 carries forward** to the next month and is charged when it crosses the threshold.

## Past-due and the 402

If a charge fails, or you owe commission with **no valid card on file**, the organization goes **past-due**. While past-due:

- Billable flex requests are paused and return a [`402 payment_required`](/errors#payment_required).
- **Free routing (`default`, `priority`, `auto`) and all Anthropic models keep working** - only priced flex is affected.

To clear it, add or update a card on the dashboard **Billing** page, then retry.
